RFR

RFR Intersystem corporations is a room for rent service provider, founded in 2008 by Ryan Sukare and Sura Ken. This corporation has been established for 15 years.

Mission

Our goal at RFR Intersystems Co. is to effortlessly connect people and organizations with the infinite possibilities of space through our innovative intersystem booking service. We provide a safe, efficient, and client-centered platform that makes rooms affordable for everyone.

Vision

By pushing the boundaries of innovation and sustainability, we aspire to become the trusted gateway to booking intersystem travel, sparking a new era of collaboration, discovery, and purpose of helping people accomplish their plans and goals.
